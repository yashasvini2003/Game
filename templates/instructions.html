Mind's Eye Game

Instructions:

  1. You will be shown a sequence of numbers on the screen.
  2. Memorize the numbers carefully as they will disappear after a few seconds.
  3. After the numbers disappear, write down the numbers in the sequence you saw them.
  4. Try to recall the numbers accurately and in the correct order.
  5. Once you have written down your answer, submit it to see your score.
  6. Your score will be based on how accurately you recalled the sequence of numbers.

Tips:

  • Focus and concentrate during the memorization phase.
  • Try to visualize the sequence in your mind.
  • Take your time to write down the numbers accurately.

Have fun!!!
